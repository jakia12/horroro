Fast forward to the age of the blockchain, and Hororo’s essence reemerged — reimagined, remixed, and viral. Milady Maker, the Ethereum NFT collection that defined a cultural moment, owes its aesthetic and emotional DNA to this obscure moe fairy. The wide, vacant eyes. The pastel void. The strange mix of innocence, irony, and emotional distance — all trace back to Hororo.
